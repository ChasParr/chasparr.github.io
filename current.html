Community Garden

Group size: Solo
Dates: Spring 2017 - Ongoing
Role: game mechanics, art, server-client, real time multiplayer

Watering plants makes them grow over time and also gives users "Karma" which is used as a currency. In a final version of the game Karma would also be purchasable through microtransactions, represented here by the buttons at the bottom.
